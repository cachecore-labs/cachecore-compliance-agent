SUPPLIER AGREEMENT NO. SA-2024-0471

This Supply Agreement is entered into as of March 15, 2024, by and between Apex Manufacturing Corp. ("Supplier") and GlobalTech Solutions Inc. ("Buyer").

1. TERM: This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue for a period of 36 months, unless earlier terminated as provided herein.

2. PAYMENT TERMS: Buyer shall pay all undisputed invoices within Net 30 days of receipt. Late payments shall accrue interest at 1.5% per month. Supplier shall submit invoices within 10 business days of delivery.

3. LIABILITY: Each party's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed $1,000,000. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages.

4. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon 60 days' prior written notice. Termination for cause requires 30 days' written notice and an opportunity to cure.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY: Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information received from the other party for 3 years following disclosure. Confidential information may only be shared with employees who have a need to know.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: All intellectual property created by Supplier in performing services under this Agreement shall be owned by Buyer upon full payment.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Any dispute shall first be submitted to mediation. If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

8. FORCE MAJEURE: Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, war, or pandemic.

9. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
