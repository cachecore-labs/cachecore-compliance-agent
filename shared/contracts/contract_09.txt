SUPPLIER AGREEMENT NO. SA-2024-1269

This Supply Agreement is entered into as of August 18, 2024, by and between Crestline Solutions Inc. ("Supplier") and MidWest Industrial Partners LLC ("Buyer").

1. TERM: The initial term of this Agreement is 12 months. After expiration, the Agreement converts to a month-to-month arrangement terminable by either party with 30 days' notice.

2. PAYMENT TERMS: Buyer shall pay invoices within Net 45 days of delivery confirmation. Supplier shall provide detailed itemized invoices. Buyer may withhold payment for disputed line items pending resolution.

3. LIABILITY: Total aggregate liability for both parties is limited to $500,000. All warranty claims must be submitted within 90 days of delivery. Consequential and incidental damages are mutually waived.

4. TERMINATION: During the initial term, termination for convenience requires 60 days' notice plus a termination fee equal to 3 months of average monthly spend. Termination for cause requires written notice with a 21-day cure window.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY: Confidential information shall be protected using commercially reasonable measures for 2 years after disclosure. Each party may retain one archival copy for legal compliance purposes.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: Work product IP vests in Buyer. Supplier is granted a non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use deliverables solely for portfolio and marketing purposes with Buyer's prior written approval.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Disputes shall be submitted to non-binding mediation. If unresolved within 45 days, either party may file suit in the state or federal courts located in Minneapolis, Minnesota.

8. FORCE MAJEURE: Force majeure includes natural disasters, strikes, and government regulations. The non-performing party must demonstrate commercially reasonable efforts to mitigate the impact.

9. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Minnesota.
