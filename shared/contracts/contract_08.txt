SUPPLIER AGREEMENT NO. SA-2024-1158

This Supply Agreement is entered into as of November 3, 2024, by and between Keystone Services Ltd. ("Supplier") and Western Pacific Holdings Corp. ("Buyer").

1. TERM: This Agreement shall remain in effect for 36 months. The Agreement may be renewed by mutual written consent for additional one-year periods.

2. PAYMENT TERMS: Buyer shall make quarterly advance payments due on the first business day of each quarter. Each quarterly payment equals 25% of the annual contract value. Overdue amounts accrue interest at the prime rate plus 2%.

3. LIABILITY: Neither party's aggregate liability shall exceed $3,000,000. The limitation of liability does not apply to obligations arising from intellectual property indemnification or intentional misconduct.

4. TERMINATION: Buyer may terminate for convenience at any time upon 90 days' written notice. Supplier may terminate only for cause. Cause includes non-payment for 60 consecutive days.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY: All non-public information exchanged under this Agreement shall be treated as confidential. Obligations survive for 7 years after the date of disclosure regardless of termination.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: Supplier assigns to Buyer all rights, title, and interest in deliverables including patents, copyrights, and trade secrets. Supplier waives moral rights to the fullest extent permitted by law.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Any controversy shall be resolved exclusively by arbitration in Seattle, Washington under JAMS Comprehensive Arbitration Rules. Discovery shall be limited to document requests.

8. FORCE MAJEURE: Neither party shall be liable for failure to perform due to events including but not limited to earthquakes, floods, government actions, or cyberattacks. Notice must be given within 48 hours.

9. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of Washington.
