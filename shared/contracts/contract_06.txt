SUPPLIER AGREEMENT NO. SA-2024-0936

This Supply Agreement is entered into as of February 28, 2024, by and between Atlas Precision Corp. ("Supplier") and Northern Alliance Ltd. ("Buyer").

1. TERM: This Agreement is effective for 18 months from the Effective Date with no automatic renewal. Extensions require a written amendment signed by both parties.

2. PAYMENT TERMS: Buyer shall pay all invoices within Net 30 days of acceptance. Acceptance occurs 5 business days after delivery unless Buyer provides a written rejection. A 3% discount applies for payment within 7 days.

3. LIABILITY: Supplier's aggregate liability shall not exceed 150% of total fees paid under this Agreement. Buyer's liability is limited to $1,500,000. Neither party excludes liability for death or personal injury caused by negligence.

4. TERMINATION: Either party may terminate for convenience upon 45 days' written notice. Upon termination, Buyer shall pay for all accepted deliverables and work in progress.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY: Confidential information shall be protected for 3 years following termination. Exceptions include information that becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving party.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: All pre-existing IP remains with its original owner. New IP created jointly shall be co-owned with each party having an unrestricted right to use and license.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Disputes shall be resolved through arbitration administered by the International Chamber of Commerce. The seat of arbitration shall be London, England.

8. FORCE MAJEURE: The affected party must notify the other within 10 days of a force majeure event. Performance is excused only for the duration of the event and a reasonable recovery period.

9. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of England and Wales.
