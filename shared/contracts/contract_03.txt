SUPPLIER AGREEMENT NO. SA-2024-0693

This Supply Agreement is entered into as of January 10, 2024, by and between Sterling Components Inc. ("Supplier") and Pacific Rim Enterprises LLC ("Buyer").

1. TERM: This Agreement is effective for 48 months from the Effective Date. Early termination is subject to the provisions of Section 4.

2. PAYMENT TERMS: Buyer shall pay invoices within Net 45 days of receipt. Supplier may charge a late fee of $250 per month on overdue balances. All payments shall be in US dollars via wire transfer.

3. LIABILITY: Supplier's total aggregate liability shall not exceed the total fees paid by Buyer in the 12 months preceding the claim. Buyer's liability is uncapped. Neither party waives liability for fraud or willful misconduct.

4. TERMINATION: Buyer may terminate for convenience upon 90 days' written notice and payment of a $25,000 early termination fee. Termination for cause requires 30 days' notice with a 20-day cure period.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY: Confidential information must be protected for 2 years following termination. Each party may disclose to professional advisors bound by equivalent confidentiality obligations.

6. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY: All deliverables and related intellectual property shall be assigned to Buyer upon creation. Supplier grants a perpetual license to Buyer for any background IP incorporated into deliverables.

7. DISPUTE RESOLUTION: Disputes shall first be escalated to senior executives for 30 days of good-faith negotiation. If unresolved, disputes shall proceed to litigation in the courts of California.

8. FORCE MAJEURE: A force majeure event excuses performance for the duration of the event. The affected party must provide notice within 5 business days of the event.

9. GOVERNING LAW: This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of California.
